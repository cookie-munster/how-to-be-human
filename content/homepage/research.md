I am interested in understanding environmental change and am fascinated by the big concepts of space, place & environment in the discipline of geography. 

I am a public scholar and work collaboratively alongside community groups and policy-makers.  Examples include:
- Community oriented climate science & translation, such as the Climate Trends Chapter of the [South Carolina Strategic Statewide Resilience and Risk Reduction Plan](https://scor.sc.gov/resilience) and the Southeast Region Explainer on the [U.S. Climate Resilience Toolkit](https://cpo.noaa.gov/US-Climate-Resilience-Toolkit-Rolls-Out-Southeast-Region/)
- Laying the groundwork for a greenbank in South Carolina by completing a [market & feasibility report](https://scholarcommons.sc.edu/geog_facpub/231/) and assisting the North Carolina greenbank in expanding to South Carolina
- Creating educational resources for K-12 Geography including co-editing the 3rd edition of [The Atlas of South Carolina](https://doi.org/10.61162/wecc9317) and producing resources like a [Gerrymandering Storymap](https://arcg.is/1Oyj9L4) that have been viewed nearly 40,000 times.
